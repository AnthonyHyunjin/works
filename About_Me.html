BIO

The purpose of this website is to showcase my interest and progression as an architecture student and thinker. I not only wish to show architectural assignments and projects completed in a variety of mediums but also many of my personal works in visual arts.

After being trained as a visual artist for four years at Walnut Hill School for the Arts, I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree at Carnegie Mellon University School of Architecture

Aside from my works from both architecture and art, I enjoy making/watching/reviewing film, watching/playing/analyzing sports, and traveling to different parts of the world.

Thank you for visiting my website! Have a fantastic day!
